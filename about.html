About Us

Founded with craftsmanship and durability at its core, Unique Ornamental Iron Inc. has been delivering custom metalwork to homes and businesses for years. From hand-welded gates to beautiful stair railings, we work closely with clients to design, fabricate, and install high-quality ironwork that stands the test of time.

Whether you're a contractor needing reliable fabrication or a homeowner wanting to boost curb appeal, we're here to build something strong — and stunning — for you.
